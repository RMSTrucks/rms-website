## Terminal Overview

The Port of Wilmington is a full-service deep-water port located at 1 Hausel Road, Wilmington, DE 19801. Operated by Enstructure, the port handles containerized cargo, fresh fruit (the largest banana port on the East Coast), automobiles, bulk/breakbulk, and refrigerated goods.

**Services:** Stevedoring, direct discharge, cold storage (100,000+ sq ft), warehousing, trucking coordination, and logistics.

## Approach Directions

| Coming From | Route | Key Instructions |
|---|---|---|
| I-95 South (from PA) | I-95 to I-495 South | Take Exit 2 (Terminal Ave/Port of Wilmington) |
| I-95 North (from MD) | I-95 to I-495 North | Stay in right lanes, take Exit 2 |
| NJ Turnpike | Cross Delaware Memorial Bridge | First exit right (9A North), 5th light turn right onto Terminal Ave, road dead-ends at port gate |
| US 13 (local) | US 13 to Terminal Avenue | Follow signs to port |

## From I-95 South (Philadelphia Area)

1. Take I-95 South toward Wilmington
2. Exit onto I-495 South (Wilmington bypass)
3. Take Exit 2: Terminal Avenue / Port of Wilmington
4. Follow Terminal Avenue to the port gate

## From I-95 North (Maryland)

1. Take I-95 North through Delaware
2. Exit onto I-495 North (right lanes -- do not miss this exit)
3. Take Exit 2: Terminal Avenue / Port of Wilmington
4. Follow Terminal Avenue to the port gate

## From the NJ Turnpike

1. Cross the Delaware Memorial Bridge (toll collected NJ-to-DE)
2. Take the first exit right after the bridge: Route 9A North
3. At the 5th traffic light, turn right onto Terminal Avenue
4. Terminal Avenue dead-ends at the port gate

## Gate Access

RFID or appointment may be required depending on terminal operations. Contact Enstructure (port operator) at (302) 472-5400 before your first visit to confirm access requirements.

**TWIC card** is required for unescorted access to secure areas of the port.

## Connecting Ports

| Port | Route | Distance |
|---|---|---|
| Port Newark-Elizabeth (NJ) | Delaware Memorial Bridge to NJ Turnpike Exit 14 | ~90 miles |
| Port of Baltimore (MD) | I-95 South to I-695 to Broening Hwy | ~70 miles |
| Port of Philadelphia (PA) | I-95 North to Philadelphia | ~30 miles |

:::tip
The approach from the NJ Turnpike via the Delaware Memorial Bridge requires taking the first exit after the bridge. If you miss it, you will end up on I-95 South toward Maryland and must backtrack. Watch for the 9A North exit immediately after the bridge toll plaza.
:::
